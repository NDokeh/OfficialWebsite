I’ve been a hobbyist photographer for the past ten years: Street photography, Cosplay Photography, Convention Photography, covering vigils and marches, candid photography. I was also self-known as the Cosplaying Otakugrapher in the DC metropolitan area convention scene who attended AnimeUSA, Otakon, Katsucon, and MAGFest. I’ve been doing photography as a hobby since I got my first camera at the age of 17. I’ve taken thousands of photos from all over the place, mostly in the east coast. Washington DC, Baltimore, Maryland, New York, Arlington VA. I’ve done so much that it would take me months possibly a year to organize all of my photos in a searchable function.
