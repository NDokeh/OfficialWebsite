Official Release

The only official release was in CD cassete and in LC

It's worth noting that the song has never been released in any digital format including iTunes.

The closest thing to a official release of her song is in Lalo Schifrin The Dirty Harry Anthology. It matches the same melody but with a different jazzy tune than the original electronic guitar used.
